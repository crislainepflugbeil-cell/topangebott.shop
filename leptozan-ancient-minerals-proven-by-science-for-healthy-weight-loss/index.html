Understanding and Resolving Your ClickBank Hop Error

Encountering a "Hop Error" with the message "Something went wrong" can be a frustrating experience, especially when you're trying to access a product through an affiliate link. This page is designed to help you understand the core issue and provide clear steps to move forward, whether you're a customer or an affiliate.

What is a ClickBank Invalid Affiliate Information Error?

This specific error, "The link you clicked contains invalid affiliate information," indicates a problem with the tracking parameters embedded in the affiliate link. Essentially, the system cannot properly identify the affiliate who referred you, or the link itself is malformed or outdated. This prevents successful affiliate tracking and can hinder your access to the intended product page.

Common Causes of Invalid Affiliate Links

  • Malformed Affiliate Link: The URL was incorrectly copied, pasted, or generated, leading to missing or incorrect tracking IDs.
  • Outdated Affiliate Information: Old or expired affiliate links might lead to this issue as tracking parameters change.
  • Incorrect Affiliate ID: The unique identifier for the affiliate might be wrong or missing from the link.
  • Temporary System Glitch: Rarely, a transient issue with the ClickBank tracking system can cause this error.

Immediate Steps to Access Your Desired Product Website

Your main priority is likely to reach the product you intended to view or purchase. Fortunately, you can still proceed directly to the vendor's page despite the affiliate link problem.

Simply click the "Continue" option if available. This action will bypass the affiliate tracking issue and direct you straight to the product website. You will still be able to learn about or acquire the product, though the referring affiliate might not receive credit for the referral.

Affiliate Marketing Troubleshooting: Preventing Future ClickBank Issues

If you are an affiliate and your links are generating this Hop Error, it's critical to address it. This error directly impacts your potential commissions due to failed affiliate tracking.

  • Verify Your Links: Always double-check your generated ClickBank affiliate links for accuracy. Ensure your correct affiliate ID is present and properly formatted.
  • Test Your Funnel: Regularly test your entire marketing funnel, from promotional material to the final product page, to ensure all links function correctly.
  • Use Official Link Tools: Generate your links using ClickBank's official link creation tools to minimize formatting errors.
  • Contact Support: If you've verified your links and the problem persists, contact ClickBank support for assistance with resolving affiliate link problems.
